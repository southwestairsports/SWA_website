Minari specifications

Many of the specifications for the Minari are the same as for the Top 80.

Cylinder head temperature – max 200ºC - 220ºC (390ºF - 428ºF).
Spark plug – NGK BR8ES (cold weather); BR9ES (hot weather) This spark plug is a resistor type in order to suppress ignition noise.  The B8ES or B9ES
     can also be used without any problems.
Spark plug gap – 0.6mm-0.7mm (0.024" - 0.028")  The plug should be replaced every 25 hours.
Torque values – refer to page EN17 of the Minari User Guide
