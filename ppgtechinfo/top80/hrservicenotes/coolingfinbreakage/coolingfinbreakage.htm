Cooling box fin breakage

After 100 hours or so, pilots will notice that the fins may be separated from the rest of the cooling box.  The intense vibration of the Top 80 causes metal fatigue.  The way to fix this is to prevent the fins from becoming, essentially, free moving tuning forks.

The way to prevent this is to apply heavy duty RTV silicone cement as shown in the photo below.  The best time to do this is when the cylinder is removed from the engine.  Otherwise, you may have to glob the cement on the exhaust side of the engine.  The surfaces must be 100% free of any oil or grease.  This is easily done with copious amounts of brake cleaner.  Let the engine sit overnight.
