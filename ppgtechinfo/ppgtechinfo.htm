Service, repair, and shipping your equipment to us

We service all types of paramotors including the Top 80, Polini, Minari, and Simonini.  We are experts on the Walbro carburetor.  Engines, or any part thereof, can be shipped to us for repair and service.  Please contact us for more information.  If you decide to have us repair or service your equipment, please follow these instructions.
