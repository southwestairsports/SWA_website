 Towing on NM Hwy 9 July 29, 2010

This was a day for towing on Hwy 9! Tow operator Had Robinson, assistants David Turner and Chuck, and pilots Lee Boone and Lee Baker were present. Both Lee's got up successfully. Lee Boone hooked a thermal at 1K' and went 20 miles across the desert, landing north of Mt. Riley. Photos by David Turner & Lee Baker.
