 Mt. Riley Expedition
October 26, 2009
Pilots Chad Bastian & Had Robinson
Tanner Sanderson, sherpa

We were privileged to have master instructor and pilot Chad Bastian in town for a few days.  Weather this week was not the best for flying so we had to find a window when flying would work.  Monday afternoon seemed good so we set out.  It takes a little less than an hour to reach the south base of Mt. Riley from west El Paso, TX.  The three of us left the truck at the base and started the 45 minute hike up to the top.  Tanner was such a help to us by carrying one of the wings.  He also took some great photos of the pilots flying.  The weak east wind did not last long but the house thermals were active, especially the one that comes off a little dark knoll about 1/2 mile south of the base.  Photos follow.  A three minute video is here and is also linked to our video page.
