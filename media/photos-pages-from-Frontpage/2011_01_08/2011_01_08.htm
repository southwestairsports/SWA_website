Sod Farm Training - "Fly City" - January 8, 2011

January 8 - Saturday - It was "Fly City" at the sod farm today.  The following pilots were present:  Doak Hoover, Bill Cummings, Lee Boone, Lee Baker, Denae Nemanic, Guillermo Vargas, & Glenn Tupper.  Visitors were Guillermo's friend, Daniel and Lee Boone's dad, Larry Boone.  Conditions were mild until about 11:15AM when strong SW winds and gusting began.

Bill Cummings looks on as Lee Boone launches

Below, Lee Boone successfully launched and flew his hang glider.  The white line to the right of the photo is the Dyneema tow line going across the field to a turn-a-round pulley 1/4 mile away and then back to Lee's glider.
