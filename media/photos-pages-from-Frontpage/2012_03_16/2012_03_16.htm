06/06/16

 

March 16 Friday – Gangbusters at the Sod Farm & at Agave Hill –   Friday was one of those days we dream about:  Strong air and a perfect lapse rate aloft.  In ordinary language, this means that we have enough wind for good ridge lift and that the cooling of the atmosphere with altitude is good for thermals to rise with low turbulence.  In the morning, Glenn Tupper and Had Robinson practiced PPG slalom with two pylons we set up.  In the afternoon, we were joined by Lee Boone for some outstanding soaring in Franklin Mountains State Park.  We all ended the day just after dusk and landed at the Coronado Country Club.  We were greeted by Jaime Lowenberg and his excited children as we touched down.  Apparently, we had provided quite a show for those enjoying an evening dinner overlooking our landing zone.  After the day, the pilots along with Erica Boone, Marilyn Robinson, and Greg Rollans headed for great barbecue and drinks at Rudy's.  All photos by Had Robinson except as noted.
