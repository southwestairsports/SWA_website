 Tuesday - March 16 - First Woman to Fly from the top of Riley

Gingher Leyendecker, visiting PG pilot and art professor at Mesa Community College, Mesa Arizona, became the first woman to launch from the summit of Mt. Riley today.  Husband Jim MacKenzie (below) and local guide Had Robinson joined Gingher on this historic occasion.  Jim & Gingher set off from the parking area near the west base of Riley. 

Jim surveys the clear summit of Riley -- there are few peaks that are as conducive to soaring as this one.

Below, our heroine is showing off her muscle.  Actually, PG is more about skill but it did take some endurance to make the 1,000' vertical hike to launch -- with all of our gear.  Congrats Gingher!

Gingher & Jim taking a break just before launch.

Gingher & Jim both had safe flights from the top, even enjoying the mix of thermal and ridge lift Riley offers.
