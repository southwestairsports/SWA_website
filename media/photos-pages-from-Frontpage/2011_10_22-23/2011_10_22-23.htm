06/06/16

 

October 22-23, 2011 – Amigo Airsho – Local pilots Glenn Tupper & Lee Boone were among the stars of our annual air show held at the Ft. Bliss Airfield, El Paso, Texas.  Glenn and Lee impressed the crowds with aerial maneuvers including synchronized flying, smoke trails, and trailing 700' long streamers behind their paramotors – something unique that only the slow moving paramotor can do.  Using his high resolution camera, Glenn was able to earn kudos from the Airsho organizers by taking photos of the event from the air.  Our slow moving aircraft are able to cruise low and slow without causing excessive annoyance of people on the ground – a perfect situation for aerial photography.  Our special thanks to our ground crew:  Dominic Padilla, Matt Watson, Chris Nystuen, John Shaw, and Tirzah Robinson.  Without them our debut at the Airsho would not have been possible.  Thank you, Paramania for supplying Glenn's glider!
