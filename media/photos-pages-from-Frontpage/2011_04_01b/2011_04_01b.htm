 April 01 Friday – April Fool's Day –   Nothing particularly foolish happened, thankfully.  But we did have a lot of great photos of three new student pilots that went up that day for a single lesson.  Michelle Davila, Mark Lenox, and Ann Jesse.  The hardest thing for all pilots to do is to keep a clear head while flying.  It is such an unusual experience that everyone, to some extent, gets disoriented.  Very few are not deeply moved by the change from man to bird.  Here are some photos of the grand event.  Below, Mark prepares for flying in the simulator.   (All photos by Ann Jesse and Mark Lenox)
