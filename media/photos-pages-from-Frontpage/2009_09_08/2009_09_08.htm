 Mt. Riley Storm

September 8, 2009

The mountain is an enormous thermal generator which makes storms in this region happen here first, generally.  This one was particularly beautiful.

In the photo below, Riley is the darker mountain in the middle way in the distance.

 

When driving in arroyos, always be careful as they can suddenly flood - as here!

Late in the day, things calmed down - humidity was high.  The little peak to the left of the summit is a good source of air going up when soaring the Mt.
