 Steve Cortez tackles Agave Hill in extreme conditions with a hang glider

Hang gliding pilot Steve Cortez, Albuquerque, NM, does a masterful job flying Agave Hill, Franklin Mountains State Park, El Paso, TX. Conditions were very strong plus the jet stream was overhead churning the atmosphere right down to the ground. The pilot does an expert job of controlling the glider when it hits the “elevator” just in front of launch.

Setting up. Steve’s mom is running the video behind him.

Ready to launch! It was blowing in about 18+ and gusting to 24.  The jet stream was overhead causing the conditions at Agave to be extreme.

Safely landed after an epic flight – Agave Hill has one of the largest LZ’s anywhere….
