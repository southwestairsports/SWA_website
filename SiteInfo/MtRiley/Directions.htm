Mt. Riley Directions

Turn off of Hwy 136 (Pete Domenici Hwy) onto Strauss Road and go to the end of pavement where it turns into A17.  Go 2.75 miles to A14 and make a left.  Go about 13.4 miles to A08.  Make a right on A08 and follow it for 2.6 miles.  Take left at the fork of A08 onto A10 and 1 mile were it dead ends with A07.  Make a left on A07.

 Follow the A07 until these coordinates 31.902149° -107.053897°.  Turn north on the jeep trail.  Go approximately 1.5 miles and turn right (northeast) at 31.918034° -107.070848°.  Follow this meager jeep trail northeast for about 1/4 mile (or as far as possible) to 31.921678° -107.069424°.  Hike northeast adjacent (north) to the arroyo for 1/4 mile to the cow trail.  Turn right (southeast) on the cow trail and follow for about 0.10 mile.  Head straight up the southwest face of Riley to the top.  There is a sort of trail and maybe some of our markers on the bushes.
