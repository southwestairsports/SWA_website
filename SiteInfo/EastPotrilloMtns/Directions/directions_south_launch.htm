Directions to the Bill Cummings (south) launch, East Potrillo Mountains

Take Exit 8 on I-10 (Artcraft Road/Pete Domenici Hwy 136/178) and go west and, after a few miles, south for a total of 9.5 miles.  Make a right on Hwy 9 (the road to Columbus, NM) and go 16 1/4 miles west and make a right on County Rd A08 (dirt).  Go north .5 miles and make a left on County Rd A03 and go .95 miles and make a right.  It is about 1.75 miles through the desert and up the west face of the hill to launch on the ridge.  It is 4 wheel drive access only.
