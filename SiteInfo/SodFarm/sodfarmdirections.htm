Directions to the Gardner Turfgrass Sod Farms

31.83685°  -106.70602°  Always check with us before coming out! 915-726-2698

Unless otherwise noted, we train at the 4th sod farm.

1. Take Exit 8 on I-10 (Artcraft Road/Pete Domenici Hwy 136/178) and go west and then south for about 8 miles to Strauss Rd and turn right (west) at the traffic light.

2. Continue on Straus Rd 0.5 miles and make a LEFT (south) on the dirt road (the entrance to the sod farms).

3. Continue straight 0.6 miles on the dirt road. You will arrive at the operations headquarters of the sod farm.

4. From the headquarters, continue 0.55 miles on the same dirt road that is directly next to the sod farm (#2) on your right. You will follow the sod farm on your right in an arc of about 120 degrees and you will be traveling north right at the end.

5. Turn RIGHT and go 100 yards. You have arrived at sod farm #4, where we usually train.

Look for the windsock/streamer or a group of vehicles on the perimeter of the sod farm. We can be anywhere because of wind direction.

DO NOT DRIVE ON THE TURF!

To order turf:  Call Gardner Turfgrass in Santa Teresa NM at (575) 589-0401
