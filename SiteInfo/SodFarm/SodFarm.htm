Gardner Turfgrass Sod Farms

 31.83685° -106.70602° 4,081' MSL

Directions to the Sod Farms.

A PPG and PG training-towing site and our safest and best area to train in the region.  This site is available through the generosity of Gardner Turfgrass, Inc.  First time visitors will need a guide – please contact us as there are hazards at the site (watch dogs, power lines, irrigation equipment, etc.)  Do not drive any vehicles on the grass. Please stay out of the way of all workmen and their equipment.

Users may always send a note of thanks to the Manager, Gardner Turfgrass, Inc. P.O. Box 18 Santa Teresa, NM 88008.
