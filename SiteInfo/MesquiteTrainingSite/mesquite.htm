Mesquite Training Hill

32° 11.879'N   106° 40.323'W

Rated H/P1 but used almost exclusively by new hang gliding pilots.  A much better training hill is Black Mountain because the winds are reliable.  We rarely use this site because of the effects of the Valley on wind direction and speed.

Flyable when winds are from the SW only.  Most of the time, the effect of the cool Rio Grande Valley makes the site unreliable because of light and variable winds.  If forecast winds are strong (10-14), they might be strong enough to overcome the "valley effect."

This is a "bunny hill" for first time pilots and beginners. The LZ is huge and unobstructed.  It faces SW with about a 75' rise. Perfect for practicing forward inflations. Kilbourne is more fun but it's a long drive to get the pilot in the LZ and then back to launch.  We share this with off-road people so it's torn up and there is trash.  We clear and clean when we go out there – a slow process.  This is BLM land so pilots can camp here if they wish.  Services are nearby on the Interstate highway.  Contact us or the RGSA for directions to this site.
