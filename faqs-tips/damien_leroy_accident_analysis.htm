Analysis of Damien LeRoy's PPG accident

by Had Robinson

This is an analysis of a powered paragliding accident that occurred in the summer of 2016 on the Florida coast.  The pilot, Damien LeRoy, was fortunate he was not killed.  Here is the ABC News report that includes the footage that Mr. LeRoy took of his flight and that of another beach visitor who witnessed part of the accident.

The video begins with the pilot doing a clockwise spiral dive.  Near the end of this sequence, he eases off the right brake in order to leave the dive.  The next thing we see are the twisted-up lines of his glider while he struggles to get at the brake handles.  The next scene is his jump into the trees.
