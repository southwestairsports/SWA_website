The Rio Grande Soaring Association is dedicated to the sport of hang gliding and paragliding in southern New Mexico and West Texas.

We have many launch sites in both states. We conduct platform truck towing (HG) from the Las Cruces city airport, pay-in towing from the Gardiner Turf Grass farms near Santa Teresa, NM and pay-out towing on NM Hwy 9.

Our main flying site is Dry Canyon which is just east of Alamogordo. It includes a 5 acre LZ which we lease from the city of Alamogordo.

The RGSA works with the city of Alamogordo, the Lincoln National Forest, and Franklin Mountains State Park of El Paso, TX to keep our local flying sites open. The RGSA is a chapter member (#41) of the United States Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association.  We carry 3rd party liability insurance provided by USHPA to protect our flying sites.
