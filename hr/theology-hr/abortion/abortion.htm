The orthodox Christian theology of Hadley Robinson

Abortion defined by some Christian leaders

by Hadley Robinson

Abortion – The shedding of innocent blood as a result of sexual wantonness with an utter disregard of the purpose of human sexuality.  A nation that permits abortion can permit anything, however barbaric and cruel.  China, Vietnam, and Russia, like the U.S., also have no limits on when and how an abortion is performed.  How tragic that we share such a legacy with nations that are among the most violent in history. 

Albert Mohler – On the 30th anniversary of the Supreme Court's infamous decision, Dr. Mohler, president of the Southern Baptist Theological Seminary, wrote an essay on how the American elite who inherited a Christian civilization have been ransacking the culture for decades.  The abortion decision by the Court was just one more result of this cultural erosion.

Billy Graham – During an interview by Joan Lunden on Good Morning America Sept. 19, 1991, Mr. Graham not only waffled (again) concerning abortion but gave the anti-Christian bigots of the cultural elite a powerful weapon to help shatter the unity of the churches involved in the fight to end the mass killings.  Mr. Graham – without qualification – stated that abortion may be necessary on occasion.  If the venerable Dr. Graham thinks abortion is necessary, it must be.  It is tragic that a national Christian leader like Mr. Graham was incapable of giving a Christian position on abortion.  Glib statements of his like, "...I came to preach the gospel, not abortion" demonstrate that his "gospel" is some other gospel.  It is because of immorality, abortion, and a host of other sin that Jesus Christ had to die – that we would need the Gospel in the first place.

Mother Teresa – She filed this brief with the Supreme Court asking that they protect the rights of a father who was arrested attempting to stop the killing of his unborn child.  "America needs no words from me...."  The Court, of course, refused to review the case – another pyrrhic victory for the feminists and the emasculated.

Paul Greenberg of the Weekly Standard comments on the evils spread by the abortion mentality: The Culture of Death.

Sydna Maas shares how she recovered from the horror on an abortion:  Breaking Sexual Soul Ties.  Every woman should read this deeply personal journey of someone who overcame the nightmare of guilt that often overwhelms those who have killed their own children.
