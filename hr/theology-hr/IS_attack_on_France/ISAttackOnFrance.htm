Islamic State's attack on France

by Hadley Robinson

I am so sorry for the mayhem that the IS created in Paris.  The French are suffering so at this time.  May the Holy Spirit be merciful and comfort those who have had their lives turned upside down.
 
Pasted below is the English version of the IS statement (from here).  Has anyone read the whole?
 
If one considers how many dedicated Communists it took to eventually overcome Czarist Russia (less than a dozen), it makes this statement worth a read.    Note the absence of a fear of death and a willingness to gladly die for the cause for which they fight.   Sun Tzu, one of the greatest generals who ever lived (300 B.C.), commented in his “Art of War” that good military strategy must avoid putting the opposing army in a corner because such men have nothing to lose and are the most ferocious fighters there are.  “Give them the option of retreat…” Tzu says.  IS behaves like a cornered army.
 
Meanwhile, the myth that Islam is peaceful continues to be spread by world leaders in the West.  They have demonstrated that defending socialist/humanist utopian dreams is more important than national security when the two are in conflict.
 
IS particularly targeted the Bataclan concert hall.  I wondered, “Why did IS target this place?”  What was going on there that was so offensive?
 
I learned that it was not just some Joan Baez peacenik concert.  It was a live performance by some outfit called “Eagles of Death Metal.”  Who are they?   I knew I would likely have to go trolling through sewage to find out.  So here goes….
 
Here is the cover from their latest album, “Zipper Down.” The album’s producer, Joshua Homme, wrote this trope promoting it, “…One should not zipper up, they should zipper down and let it all hang out.”  No doubt, a great majority here and in Europe wholeheartedly agree.

 
 
 
Are Islamic State and a few others among the miniscule that are offended by the lewd and vulgar culture in which we live?   Could it be described as “…a profligate prostitution party?”
 
It seems to be so….
 
IS’s quote of Al-Hashr 2 echoed something found in the Bible, not surprisingly.  Jeremiah 5:15-17,
 
O house of Israel," declares the LORD, "I am bringing a distant nation against you-- an ancient and enduring nation, a people whose language you do not know, whose speech you do not understand.  Their quivers are like an open grave; all of them are mighty warriors.  They will devour your harvests and food, devour your sons and daughters; they will devour your flocks and herds, devour your vines and fig trees.  With the sword they will destroy the fortified cities in which you trust.
 
Like the ancient Assyrians and Babylonians, has Islam and IS, in particular, been sent for a Divine purpose?  Or, are they some manifestation of random evil which we have to endure in a fallen world?  With the former, we would end up fighting against God.  With the latter, we are not fighting men, but an idea.  If so, how is that done?  What defense can be plucked from the abyss of human decadence that is now the West?
 
It is distressing that the highest Court in our land is content with the state of our culture and does everything possible to drive it further into a bottomless Pit.  Where will it all go?  We are in the firm grip of the international Left which has effectively suppressed any discussion of the alarming state of affairs we are in.  Even so, it is difficult to have a dialogue with Hell….
 
Islamic fighters are not easy to suppress.  It’s like getting rid of mushrooms in a manure pile by plucking them out.  The next morning there are ten more….
